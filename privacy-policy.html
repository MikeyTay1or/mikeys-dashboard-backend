Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

Mikey's Dashboard is a personal analytics tool used solely by its owner, Mikey Taylor, to monitor social media performance data from connected accounts. This app is not available to the general public and does not collect data from third parties.

Data collected

This app accesses the following data from connected social media accounts solely for the purpose of displaying analytics to the account owner:

Instagram: profile information, post insights, follower counts, reach, and engagement metrics.

Facebook: page insights and engagement data.

How data is used

All data accessed through this app is used exclusively by the app owner to view their own social media performance. No data is sold, shared, or distributed to any third parties.

Data storage

No user data is stored permanently. All data is fetched in real time and displayed only to the authenticated owner of the connected accounts.

Third party access

This app does not share any data with third parties. It is a private tool for personal use only.
